Really, really Good Coffee ☕️

For a while now, Monica(co-founder) and I talked about ideas on how to sell coffee, which is our passion and many other’s passion. It started when we spoke about how many authentic Colombian coffee farmers there are and how she could sell coffee at a farmer’s market in Colombia. Unfortunately, there are no “real” farmers market near her like there is in Florida. This idea came to me because Saturday morning, there are people lined up for a cup of coffee. I myself was included in that line.

Our idea came alive when I struggled to find a good cup of cappuccino that wasn’t watered down by milk, didn’t have too much froth, or an espresso taste and the mere fact that I have spent hundreds of dollars in the past year just on coffee. I’ve had good coffee, a lot of bad coffee, too much creamer, not enough creamer, too weak or too strong, you name it, this coffee was created out of a need that there just is a lot of bad coffee out there and life is too short to settle forbad tasting coffee.

This is where the birth of Really Really Good Coffee comes from. Our team at RRGC, wanted to uniquely design an experience the same enjoyment we have with a cup of coffee. From the aroma to the taste and also knowing that our beans come straight from the farms in Colombia where we wanted to encourage growth in small farms and commercial farms. We also believe in the opportunity in employment and what it does to the local economy. So, lets enjoy a guilt-free cup of coffee together.

In the next year, RRGC’s goal is to have our own farm in Colombia to directly hire farmers and then invest in roasters. Then look into opening locations in the EU.

Our Official Launch Date is July 1, but we thought we’d let you in a little early.
